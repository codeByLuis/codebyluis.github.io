This is
my story

After a decade managing a customer service call center, I realized the one thing I enjoyed most was analysing data to make informed business desicions. I was using Microsoft Excel to clean and manipulate the data, then in the summer of 2021 I realized there is a entire industry that specilizes in Data Analysis and a long list of tools in the trade, so in late September of 2021 I decided to leave that job and focus on learning the tools to become a Data Analyst.

At this time I made use of my real estate license to earn an income while learning more about Data Analitics and what tools to use. Like most people, I spent hours watching YouTube videos and purchased a course to learn PowerBI because of its relation to Excel. I studied that for a while I created a dashboard making use of housing data which I have access to from the local multiple listing service. What I found is that powerBI can be quite combersome when it came to publishing and sharing my work. Then I signed up for Codecademy's "BI Data Analyst" career path and was introduced to Tableau Public and fell in love with it's ease of use for publishing and sharing my work so I have been using Tableau Public as I continue to learn and evolve in this field.

From my experience as a Customer Service Manager, I have the experienced in making use of data to drive continuous improvement. I have a knack for identifying areas where business processes can be improved, plus developing and implementing solutions that make a real difference. I am a highly motivated and results-oriented individual, passionate about learing new skills and using data to improve business processes while creating a positive customer experience.

I am looking forward to an exciting and properous career in the field of Data Analitics.
