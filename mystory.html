This is
my story

After more than a decade managing a customer service call center, I realized the one thing I enjoyed most was analysing data to make informed business desicions. I was using Microsoft Excel for extraction, transformation and loading of data plus exploratory data analysis, then in the summer of 2021 I realized there is a entire industry that specilizes in Data Analysis and a long list of tools for the trade. This only caused me to become more interested in the field and in late September of 2021 I decided to leave that job and focus on learning more about becoming a Data Analyst.

Having past experience with real estate sales, I made use of my real estate license to earn an income while making the transition. Like most people, I spent hours watching YouTube videos and purchased a course to learn PowerBI, it seemed like the natural thing because of its relation to Excel. I studied that for a while and created a dashboard making use of housing data from the local multiple listing service. What I found is that powerBI can be quite combersome when it comes to publishing and sharing my work without paying for the Pro version. Then I signed up for Codecademy's "BI Data Analyst" career path and was introduced to Tableau Public. I fell in love with it's ease of use for publishing and sharing dashboards so I have been using Tableau Public as I continue to learn and evolve in this field.

From my experience as a Customer Service Manager, I have the experienced in making use of data to drive continuous improvement, identifying areas where business processes can be improved, plus developing and implementing solutions that make a real difference. I am a highly motivated and results-oriented individual, passionate about learing new skills and using data to improve business processes while creating a positive customer experience.

I am constantly learning and I look forward to an exciting career in the field of Data Analitics.
